Behind the name "Charas Lounge" stands italo-german music composer, producer and audio engineer Ramon Giannini based in Munich. .

He began his production journey in 1997, exploring the realms of Hip Hop and Dancehall, influenced by artists like DJ Krush, Massive Attack, Tricky and The RZA

Recent Releases / Productions

  • Sound design for STUDIO760 (KIA, Broke Today, Marlon Lipke)
  • EP and Single/RMX Productions for German underground rapper GNATO
  • New Album Release: "Round Robin" - 19 instrumental tracks, 60 minutes playtime. A journey through Classic Lounge, Chillhop, TripHop, and experimental vibes.
  • Reggae Production: "Perilous Crime" for Jamaican artist Dez I Boyd under Full100Music Label
  • "The System" EP for Cellz (South African/German vocalist and saxophone player) + 2 more singles: Trust feat. Black Pepper & Go/Stay
  • Reggae Instrumental EP #1 & #2 with Gambian artist Jarck Boy + 5 tracks on his Reggae Album We Are All One People under Full100Music Label
  • Since 2018: Keyboard Player in the Munich-based international Live-HipHop Band D!aspora

Charas Lounge intends with his music to join together various genres, styles, and artists for a relaxing and emotional listening experience.
