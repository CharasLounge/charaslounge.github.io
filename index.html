Behind the name "Charas Lounge" stands italo-german music composer, producer and audio engineer Ramon Giannini based in Munich.

He began his production journey in 1997, exploring the realms of Hip Hop and Dancehall, influenced by artists like DJ Krush, Massive Attack, Tricky and The RZA.

From soulful lounge arrangements, classic boombap tracks to experimental soundscapes with jazzy elements…his musical production isn't limited to only one genre.


“After silence, that which comes nearest to expressing the inexpressible is music.”

Aldous Huxley
